About Me

Who am I?

My name is Andrew and I love exploring! Whether it be hiking in the mountains, searching through a new data set, or touring small villages on Google Earth, I'm naturally curious and am always looking to find something new.

I live in Seattle, Washington and work as a Data Engineer. My primary role is building a data warehouse to help my business make data-driven decisions. I build data pipelines, create dashboards, and write code to automate processes. In my free time, I love sailing, traveling, skiing, and bike riding.

Before Seattle, I spent four years living in Portland, Oregon where I worked as a structural engineer. I used teamwork, math, and computers to design the new buildings and retrofits for existing buildings. Many of the skills required for structural engineering translate over to Data Analytics: Both require managing projects, communicating with stakeholders, creating graphics to show information visually, cleaning data, and explaining your results. My experience on $100M+ projects gave me confidence to take on big challenges without feeling overwhelmed.

Reach out by email or LinkedIn if you want to chat about any of my projects. Links to both are at the bottom of this page.

Thanks for exploring my site, and I hope you learn something new!
